What Street Art Says About a City’s Soul
Street art, once dismissed as vandalism, is now recognized as a powerful form of urban expression. From political commentary to bold murals celebrating culture, street art transforms public spaces and reflects the identity of a community. Artists often use their work to challenge authority, question norms, or simply beautify neglected spaces.

Cities like Berlin, São Paulo, and Melbourne have become known for their vibrant street art scenes. By walking through these urban galleries, people can experience raw, unfiltered stories from the streets themselves.
