The Future of AI
The future of AI holds vast potential to transform how we live and work. Advances in AI could lead to breakthroughs in science, improve global health, and solve complex societal problems. However, it also brings uncertainties around job automation, ethical use, and societal impacts.

Ongoing research aims to create AI systems that are not only intelligent but also safe, explainable, and aligned with human values. The collaboration between humans and AI will likely shape the next era of innovation.
